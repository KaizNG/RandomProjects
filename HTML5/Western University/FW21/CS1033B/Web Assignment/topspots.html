Top Spots

Obviously, Western's campus is absolutely huge, so there's probably a bunch of spots that people like going to. I mean, the campus is beautiful, with tons and tons of buildings and tons of places to discover. Like I found out not too long ago that we have a power plant on our campus. Anyways, my favourite spots for others to discover on campus are as follows.


Favourite place to get food at night when the cafeteria food doesnt slap as hard as it usually does.

This one's kind of a grey area in terms of its campus-ness, but it's pretty great having a Subway, or any fast food place really.
When you don't feel like eating cafe food, having another good source of legit food is always great. Plus, because it's a little
further than the UCC, there's usually not that long of a line, which, when you're pretty much starving, is a really nice bonus
for maybe a bit more of a walk.


Favourite place to stand outside for an hour or more at 2 in the morning because someone pulled the fire alarm.

Home sweet home right? This one might not apply to you if you live at a hall that isn't called The Zoo. All jokes aside, I love
this place sometimes,it's home, I get to sleep and eat and do all the things I want to do all in my own living space. I mean
isn't that what home is supposed to be?


Favourite place to wander.

This isn't really specific to the UCC building, but I do enjoy wandering around the area of UCC. This area makes for good
walks at night when they're IDing everyone at the hall and there's a line that goes up the street and you need something to do
in the meantime. There's also a good number of places to eat there so that's a nice bonus.
