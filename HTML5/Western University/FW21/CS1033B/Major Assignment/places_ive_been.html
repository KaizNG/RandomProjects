places i've been

Even though I'm a social reject, I've travelled to a couple of places, some a little more interesting than others.

Up until I can remember, I've been to Ireland, Scotland, China, the United States, and Hong Kong. Weirdly enough I can't find any images of my travels there aside from Scotland, Google Drive does me dirty sometimes so it's probably in some hard drive at my house.

Scotland and Ireland are part of a completely different story, a story which you can find here!

Otherwise, here's a brief rundown of each location.

 


China - Kaiping Village

This is the village that my mom grew up in, back when my mom's side grandmother lived there, my family would visit every summer or two. Kaiping is really great for its street food and traditional feeling streets and atmosphere. Although it definitely isn't a place for
everyone, as it's typically pretty hot and humid and there are tons of mosquitos, it's a pretty nice trip every time we go back


United States - Seattle

A couple of years ago I went to Seattle to stay at a relative's house for a baby shower. My family stayed there for around 2 months
so we had time to socialize and spend time with the other relatives that were there. although it wasn't too different from downtown
Toronto (where I'm from), I did get the chance to get on the Seattle great wheel and I know what you're probably thinking.
"Isn't that just a regular Ferris Wheel?" no. absolutely not. it's the Seattle Great Ferris Wheel.


Hongkong

A long time ago, many years ago, I went to Hongkong on a trip to visit some relatives, it was after one of the china trips, although I fail to remember which one in particular. at the time, however, I had two brothers living in hong kong so I was able to visit both of them and their homes.
